FAQ

Common Questions

Is Genesis a gym?+
Not in the traditional sense. A gym sells equipment access. Genesis provides structured coaching, programming, accountability, and progress tracking. The training environment happens to be premium.
What happens during the assessment?+
Body composition analysis, strength evaluation, movement screening, lifestyle discussion, and a personalized roadmap. It takes 60–75 minutes and it is substantive — not a sales pitch.
Is there any obligation after the assessment?+
None. You keep your results and roadmap regardless of any decision. If there is a fit, we'll have a conversation about what a coaching engagement looks like. No pressure, no countdown.
Why only 40 members?+
Coaching quality degrades at scale. We deliberately cap membership at 40 so every member receives genuine attention from the founding team — not an overworked floor coach. This limit is real.
What makes Genesis different from other gyms in Kharadi?+
Three specialist coaches on one team — S&C, clinical nutrition, and rehabilitation — integrated into a single coaching model. Most facilities in Kharadi sell memberships. Genesis sells outcomes.
How do I know if I'm a good fit?+
If you're serious about improving your physical capacity, want expert-led structure, and are prepared to commit consistently — you're the right profile. Apply and let the process determine fit.
